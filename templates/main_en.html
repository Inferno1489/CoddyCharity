About Project

Programming school for children CODDY and CF "Gulfstream" founded charity project - help to children with developmental and health diseases, from single parent families, from low-income families,to those, who are at home school, or to orphans.
Kids from "Gulfstream" learning the basics of programming, design, etc. for free.
